## 🚀 Featured Course Projects & Case Studies

### 1. EcoStruct AI: Reinforcement Learning in Sustainable 3D Construction
* **The Problem:** 3D printing large structural materials out of concrete is highly susceptible to environmental variables, leading to catastrophic material waste and increased carbon footprints.
* **The Solution:** Proposed a closed-loop, reinforcement learning AI system integrated directly into the print-heads of large-scale 3D construction printers. 
* **Mechanism:** The AI ingests data from multi-modal sensor arrays (computer vision cameras and in-line rheometers) to dynamically adjust concrete extrusion parameters thousands of times per second.
* **Outcome:** Designed to eliminate physical material waste and accelerate the deployment of sustainable housing.

### 2. CFIN: Federated Machine Learning for Financial Integrity
* **The Problem:** Government agencies and financial institutions face massive exposure to organized fraud rings that exploit siloed, rigid rule-based monitoring systems.
* **The Solution:** Designed the architecture for a Cross-Program Fraud Intelligence Network (CFIN).
* **Mechanism:** Utilizes federated machine learning, allowing different entities to train local models on their respective datasets and share only the model weight updates to a central server. Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) map hidden relationships between synthetic identities, IP addresses, and money mules.
* **Outcome:** Maximizes fraud detection across jurisdictions while strictly preserving citizen privacy under HIPAA and the Privacy Act.

### 3. The Master Builder: AGI in the AEC Industry
* **The Problem:** The global construction industry is fragmented, leading to rampant miscommunications, budget overruns, and severe material waste.
* **The Solution:** Explored the theoretical application of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) as a centralized project manager for mega-projects.
* **Mechanism:** The system acts as the ultimate "Flatten layer," bridging the gap between digital design and physical execution by coordinating synchronized swarms of automated construction robots and recalculating structural physics in real-time.
